### 2. Switch to Spectrogram Mode

- Click the track name dropdown (small arrow next to the track title).
- Select **Spectrogram** instead of Waveform.
<img width="1917" height="848" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-19 025320" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b49e4fba-4f2c-4f63-9bf2-5d2b60e467f9" />

- Adjust spectrogram settings if needed (increase window size, change scale to log, etc.) for better visibility.

### 1. Open Audacity 

Download and open the audio file in Audacity.

![[Pasted image 20260219025247.png]]
### 2. Switch to Spectrogram Mode

- Click the track name dropdown (small arrow next to the track title).
- Select **Spectrogram** instead of Waveform. ![[Screenshot 2026-02-19 025320.png]]
- Adjust spectrogram settings if needed (increase window size, change scale to log, etc.) for better visibility.
![[Pasted image 20260219025427.png]]
### 3. Reveal the Flag  
Once in spectrogram mode, you will see text visually embedded in the higher frequencies of the audio.   The displayed text is the flag.
